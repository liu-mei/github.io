Brief Introduction

Ms. Yi Zhang currently is a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Su Rong at School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU). She received her B.S. degree at school of control science and engineering from Shandong University, China, in 2014.

Research Interests

  • urban traffic flow management

  • model-based traffic signal scheduling

  • multi-directional pedestrian-flow simulation
