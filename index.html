Brief Introduction

Mei Liu received her B.Sc. degree in Mathematics from China University of Mining and Technology in 2012, and her Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from University of Science and Technology of China in 2017. From 2017 to 2018, she worked with The University of Hong Kong (HKU) and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a research associate/assistant. She is currently a research associate in HKU. Dr. Mei Liu currently is an associate professor at Tianjin University, Tianjin, China.

Research Interests

  • Control Theory and Application of Negative Imaginary Systems

  • Positive Real System, Generalized System

  • Robust Control, Flexible Structure Control
